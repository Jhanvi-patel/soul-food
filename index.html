ABOUT US

Abhijit patel, a self-made entrepreneur set up soul food in 1983, as a small roadside sandwich shop in the lane of GLS college. He is a self-taught chef & all the recipes have been carefully curated from scratch by him ever since. New Freezeland has been vadodara's favorite spot for almost four decades now; for hanging out with family, friends or colleagues over Lip-smacking sandwiches, Coffee, and much more!
